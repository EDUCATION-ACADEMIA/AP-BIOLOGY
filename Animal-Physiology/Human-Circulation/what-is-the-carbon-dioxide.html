What is the carbon dioxide?

(A) ** its a waste products. Its a gas

(B) its blood

(C) its oxygen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A colorless, odorless gas. It is a waste product made by the body. Carbon dioxide travels in the blood from the body’s tissues to the lungs.

Concept note-2: -’CO2 is a waste, so it really has to be a cheap process that leads to an interesting component. ‘ CRI, which has worked out how to use residual gases from industries such as steel and iron making, also plans to put waste CO2 from power plants and cement factories to good use.

Concept note-3: -Carbon dioxide is a waste product of combusting fossil fuels, and its accumulation in the atmosphere presents a planetary hazard. Carbon dioxide is also managed and used as a resource.

Concept note-4: -Carbon dioxide is an end product of cellular respiration in organisms that obtain energy by breaking down sugars, fats and amino acids with oxygen as part of their metabolism. This includes all plants, algae and animals and aerobic fungi and bacteria.

Concept note-5: -The Respiratory System They work together to bring oxygen into the body and take carbon dioxide (a waste product) out.
